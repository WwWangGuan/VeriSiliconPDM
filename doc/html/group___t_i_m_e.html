◆ osal_get_uptime()

uint64_t osal_get_uptime ( void  )

Get the number of milliseconds since the operating system(or scheduler) started.

Note
Invocation is not recommended when the SysTimer interrupt is masked. For example, after entering the critical section through osal_enter_critical(). This causes the RTOS to stop tick counting, which affects the accuracy of the results returned by the function.
